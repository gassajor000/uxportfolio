Description:

Web App demo for a collaborative event planning app. BringIt! provides a central place to keep track of all the items you need for your event and see who has volunteered to bring what. BringIt! was developed by a team of 3 students over the course of a quarter. The app features a slick, Material inspired UI, refined by several rounds of usability tests.

Features:

Optimized for Mobile screens

Intuitive interaction flow

Points based incentives to motivate equal contributions

Express NodeJS backend

Boostrap and Handlebars frontend

Responsive custom built components (search sugestions box, item details pane, etc)

Google Analytics and Google Optimize integrations for user testing
